1. What is Sales AI?

Your AI-powered AEO sales consultant

Sales AI is a conversational AI tool built directly into the Signal AEO admin panel. It acts as a trained sales consultant that understands Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) — the practice of making a business visible when people ask questions on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google Gemini.


Its job is to help Signal AEO sales reps do three things faster:


  • Understand the prospect — what their business does, where they're invisible on AI engines, and what the gap costs them.
  • Build the case — using real scored data (ICE, PQS, Search Volume) to show why they need AEO right now.
  • Handle objections — with pre-trained responses for "how do you know this is real?", "we already have SEO", and "it's too expensive".
💡
AEO vs SEO — the key difference SEO gets you ranked on Google's blue links. AEO gets you recommended by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini when someone asks a question. Buyers are increasingly skipping Google and asking AI directly — AEO captures that traffic.
🔑
API Key (DeepSeek) Sales AI runs directly from your browser to DeepSeek's API. No backend involved. You need to paste a DeepSeek API key once in the Settings panel. It's saved in your browser's localStorage under the label "Sales AI Key" and persists across sessions.

3. The Chat Interface

Your always-on AI sales conversation

How the chat works

The chat area is a full AI conversation powered by DeepSeek (an OpenAI-compatible model). Every message you send is sent directly from your browser to DeepSeek's servers — no middleman.


The AI always starts with the same identity: "I'm your Signal AEO Sales AI." It has been trained (via a system prompt) with:


  • Full knowledge of what AEO is and how it differs from SEO
  • Signal AEO's source of truth methodology
  • Rules for objection handling
  • The Sales Overview format (structure for explaining why a business needs AEO)
  • Instructions to NEVER invent numbers, add filler commentary, or suggest free trials / contact CTAs

⚡ Streaming responses

The AI types its response in real time — you see each word as it arrives. This uses Server-Sent Events (SSE) so you get instant feedback without waiting for the full response.


You can stop a streaming response at any time using the ■ stop button in the input bar.

🔄 New Chat

The "New Chat" button in the header resets the conversation — starts fresh. The previous conversation is automatically saved to Chat History before it disappears.


Each chat session gets its own history entry titled from your first message.

5. Full AEO Audit

A scored, data-backed report in one click

What it is

The Full AEO Audit is a one-click AI-generated report that scores a business across three dimensions: ICE (keyword priority scoring), PQS (prompt quality score), and Required Search Volume. The result is a structured data table — not a paragraph — so you can use the numbers directly in conversations.

Audit input fields


FieldEffect on the audit
Business Name Personalises keywords and example prompt generated by the AI
Description Free-text about what they do — directly shapes which keywords DeepSeek picks
Business Type Sets the ICE weight profile (see Section 6). B2B SaaS, Local Service, Healthcare, etc.
Business Size Used to calibrate authority estimates in Confidence scoring
Number of Competitors Feeds into Competitor Density → Required Search Volume formula

What the audit returns

The AI returns a JSON report (which the UI parses and displays as cards and a table). It contains:


  • 5 ICE-scored keywords — ranked from highest to lowest priority
  • 1 Example AEO Prompt — with its PQS score and whether it meets the 4.0 threshold
  • Required Search Volume — total monthly + weekly prompts needed to compete
  • Weight label — confirms which ICE profile was applied (e.g. "B2B SaaS weights")
⚠️
Non-streaming call Unlike the chat, the Full AEO Audit uses a non-streaming API call (stream: false). This is because the AI must return complete, parseable JSON — and streaming JSON mid-chunk breaks the parser. You'll see a loading state while it processes (usually 3–8 seconds).

Three actions after the audit runs


A

Generate Sales Overview

Sends the audit data to the chat AI and asks it to write a 400-word consultant-style explanation of why this specific business needs AEO — using the real ICE and PQS numbers.

B

Discuss in Chat

Sends the audit results to the chat as a summary and asks the AI to help you build the strongest sales angle for this business. Good for follow-up strategy or objection prep.

C

Run New Audit

Clears the results and lets you re-run with different inputs — useful if you change the business type or description and want a fresh set of scores.

6. ICE Scoring — Deep Dive

How keywords get prioritised for AEO

What ICE stands for

ICE is a prioritisation framework used to rank which keywords a business should focus on first for AEO. Each keyword gets three scores from 1 to 5, then they're combined using weights based on the business type.


LetterMeaningScore rangeWhat it measures
I Impact 1 – 5 Revenue or lead potential if this keyword ranks on ChatGPT / Perplexity / Gemini. 5 = massive potential.
C Confidence 1 – 5 How likely the business can actually rank given their current authority, content, and competition. 5 = very likely.
E Effort 1 – 5 How easy it is to create the AEO content needed. 5 = easy (inverse of difficulty). 1 = very hard.

The ICE formula

# Weighted ICE score
ICE = (wi × Impact) + (wc × Confidence) + (we × Effort)

# Example — B2B SaaS: wi=0.5, wc=0.3, we=0.2
# Keyword: "custom software development Utah"
# Impact=5, Confidence=4, Effort=4
ICE = (0.5 × 5) + (0.3 × 4) + (0.2 × 4)
ICE = 2.50 + 1.20 + 0.80
ICE = 4.50 ← HIGH priority

Weight profiles — by business type

Different industries need different priorities. A local service business needs high Confidence more than raw Impact because their market is smaller. A B2B SaaS needs high Impact because each deal is worth more.


Business Type Impact (wi) Confidence (wc) Effort (we) Why
B2B SaaS 0.500.300.20 High-value deals — Impact matters most
Local Service 0.300.400.30 Ranking ability is key in a geo-limited market
B2C / E-commerce 0.400.300.30 Balanced — volume and ease both matter
Healthcare / Legal / Financial 0.450.350.20 YMYL trust requirements — authority is critical, effort is secondary
Other 0.400.300.30 Default balanced profile

Priority thresholds

ICE ScorePriorityMeaning
≥ 3.50 HIGH Focus here first — highest ROI for AEO investment
2.50 – 3.49 MEDIUM Worth optimising after high-priority keywords are covered
< 2.50 LOW Low return — address only after everything else is done

7. PQS — Prompt Quality Score

How well the business's content answers AI questions

What PQS measures

PQS (Prompt Quality Score) measures how well a business's existing content is structured to be cited by AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. It has two components:


ComponentFull nameMeasures
PC_avg Prompt Clarity Score How clearly and directly the business's content answers customer questions (structured Q&A, FAQ, clear headings)
RC_avg Response Citation Likelihood How likely AI engines are to cite or quote this business (backlinks, schema markup, E-E-A-T signals, citation history)

The PQS formula

# PQS formula — RC is weighted higher (0.6) because citation is harder to achieve
PQS = (PC_avg × 0.4) + (RC_avg × 0.6)

# Example: PC_avg = 4.00, RC_avg = 4.33
PQS = (4.00 × 0.4) + (4.33 × 0.6)
PQS = 1.60 + 2.60
PQS = 4.20 ← Threshold MET ✓

# Threshold: PQS ≥ 4.0 means content is competitive for AI citation

How to explain PQS to a prospect


💬
"Your Prompt Quality Score is 3.2 out of 5. That means when someone asks ChatGPT a question your business should answer, your content is below the threshold where AI engines start citing it confidently. Competitors scoring above 4.0 are getting recommended instead of you. That's the gap we close."

8. Required Search Volume

How many AI queries this business needs to win

What it is

Required Search Volume is an estimate of how many times per month (and per week) people are asking AI engines a question relevant to this business. It's not Google search volume — it's the estimated number of AI prompts in their category that they need to be visible for.

The three input factors

FactorValuesMeaning
Competitor Density 1 = few, 2 = moderate, 3 = heavy How many competitors are also trying to rank on AI engines in this category. More competition = more query volume exists.
YMYL Penalty 1 if healthcare / legal / financial, else 0 YMYL (Your Money Your Life) topics see more AI queries because users ask AI for advice. Adds 30 to total.
Local Advantage 1 if local service + geo keywords, else 0 Local businesses often face less national competition — subtracts 20 from the total (easier to dominate locally).

The formula

# Monthly total prompt volume estimate
Total = (Competitor_Density × 100) + (YMYL × 30) − (Local_Advantage × 20)
Weekly = ceil(Total ÷ 4)

# Example — Healthcare clinic in a competitive city
# Competitor Density=3, YMYL=1 (healthcare), Local Advantage=1
Total = (3 × 100) + (1 × 30) − (1 × 20)
Total = 300 + 30 − 20 = 310
Weekly = ceil(310 ÷ 4) = 78 queries/week

How to use this in a sales conversation

💬
"In your category, there are roughly 310 AI queries per month — about 78 per week — where people are asking ChatGPT or Perplexity which healthcare provider to choose. Right now, you're not showing up in any of them. Your competitors are. That's 78 missed opportunities every single week."

9. Sales Overview Generator

A consultant-style case built from real audit data

What it produces

The Sales Overview is a max 400-word, consultant-style explanation of why a specific business needs AEO — written using the real ICE scores, PQS, and search volume from the audit. It follows this exact structure:


  1. Business snapshot — one sentence: what they do, who they serve
  2. The core problem — why they're invisible on ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini right now (in plain language, no jargon)
  3. The gap in numbers — highest ICE keyword name + score, PQS score, how far from the threshold
  4. Why it matters now — real business impact: leads going to competitors who ARE showing up on AI
  5. What AEO fixes — how Signal AEO closes these gaps: content structure, citability, answer optimization

🚫
What it never includes No contact requests. No free trials. No "15-minute call" CTAs. No "let me know". Pure business case only — the sales rep handles the next step themselves.

10. Objection Handling Chips

One-click responses to the most common prospect pushbacks

What they are

The four chips above the chat input bar are pre-written prompts that send a specific scenario to the AI. One click — the AI immediately gives you a consultative response you can use right now on a call or in a DM.

💡 Source of truth objection
💰 Price objection
🔍 "We have SEO already"

💡 Source of truth objection

Prospect says: "How do I know this is real? Where does your data come from?"


The AI explains that all data comes from running live, real-time queries on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini using the exact questions their customers type — and that the prospect can verify it themselves in 2 minutes.

💰 Price objection

Prospect says: "AEO is too expensive."


The AI reframes the cost against the cost of being invisible — every week there are ~80+ AI queries where competitors show up and they don't. It shifts the conversation from "what does AEO cost?" to "what does invisibility cost?"

🔍 "We have SEO already"

Prospect says: "We're already doing SEO, we don't need this."


The AI explains the fundamental difference: SEO optimises for Google's blue links. AEO optimises for AI-generated answers. Buyers are increasingly skipping Google and asking AI directly — SEO doesn't capture that traffic at all.

📄 Write chat script

Sends a request to the AI to generate a Sales Overview for the business currently loaded in the audit. If no audit is loaded, the AI asks for the business details first.


Better path: run the Full AEO Audit first, then use the "Generate Sales Overview" button so the AI has real data to work with.

11. Source of Truth Protocol

The methodology behind every claim the AI makes

What it is

The Source of Truth is a block of instructions baked into the AI's system prompt. It tells the AI exactly how to respond whenever a prospect questions whether the data is real — without being defensive or using jargon.

The protocol verbatim


"Our analysis comes from running live, real-time queries on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google Gemini using the exact questions their customers type. We record what each AI actually answers: whether this business appears, and who appears instead.

The prospect can verify everything themselves in 2 minutes: open any of those AI tools, type the same question, and see the same result. We also check the website's structured data using public testing tools (Google Rich Results Test, Schema.org validator). No guesswork, no black box. Every claim is reproducible."

✅
Tone rule The AI is trained to deliver this response calmly and factually — never defensive, no jargon. It hands the proof directly to the prospect and invites them to check it themselves. That builds trust faster than any slide deck.

12. Chat History

Every conversation saved automatically

How it works

Every chat session is automatically saved to your browser's localStorage under the key sales_ai_history. No server, no database — it's stored on your computer.


  • Auto-save — triggers after each complete message (not mid-stream)
  • Title — taken from your first message in that session (first 60 characters)
  • Load — click any history entry to reload the full conversation
  • Delete single — hover the entry → trash icon → confirm dialog
  • Clear all — "Clear all" button at the top of the history panel → confirm dialog
  • New conversation — "New conversation" button at the bottom of the history panel, or "New Chat" in the header
⚠️
Browser-local storage Chat history is stored in your browser only. Clearing your browser data or switching browsers will erase it. It does not sync across devices or team members.
