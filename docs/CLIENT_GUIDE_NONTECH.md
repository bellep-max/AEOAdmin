# Signal AEO — How It Works (Plain-English Guide)

A simple guide to understanding your rankings, written for non-technical readers.
No jargon — just what the numbers mean and how to read your reports.

---

## 1. What this platform does

Search is changing. People increasingly ask **AI assistants** — ChatGPT, Google
Gemini, and Perplexity — questions like _"best medical weight loss near me."_
Those assistants recommend a handful of businesses in their answer.

**Signal AEO tracks where your business shows up in those AI answers** — and works
to move you higher. Think of it like traditional SEO, but for AI search instead
of the classic Google results page.

---

## 2. The words you'll see (and what they mean)

| Term                | Plain meaning                                                                                             |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Keyword**         | A phrase a customer might ask (e.g. _"medical weight loss"_). We track your position for each one.        |
| **Platform**        | Which AI answered: **ChatGPT**, **Gemini**, or **Perplexity**. We check all three.                        |
| **Rank / Position** | Where your business appears in the AI's answer. **#1 is best.** Lower number = better.                    |
| **Top 3**           | The goal. Being in the first three recommendations is where almost all the attention goes.                |
| **Bi-weekly run**   | We re-check every keyword **every 2 weeks** and record the new position.                                  |
| **New**             | A keyword we just started tracking (only checked once so far).                                            |
| **Ongoing**         | A keyword we're actively working to improve.                                                              |
| **Won / Locked**    | A keyword that reached **Top 3 and held it.** It "graduated" — we keep watching it to make sure it stays. |

---

## 3. How to read a keyword row

Each keyword shows its position on each AI platform, plus how it changed:

> **medical weight loss** — ChatGPT **#3 ↑**, Gemini **#4**, Perplexity **#1 ↑**

- The **number** is your current position (lower = better).
- An **↑** means you improved since the last check; **↓** means you slipped; **=** means no change.
- **Green = good news, red = needs attention.**

The detail view shows four points in time so you can see the trajectory:

| First             | Last 2 Weeks       | Current               | Change                            |
| ----------------- | ------------------ | --------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Where you started | The previous check | The most recent check | The movement between the last two |

Example: _First #7 → Last 2 weeks #2 → Current #1_ means steady improvement, now at the top.

---

## 4. The Dashboard at a glance

- **Active Clients** — businesses currently being tracked.
- **Total Keywords / Keywords with Rank** — how many phrases we track and how many are showing up in AI answers.
- **Network Health** — behind-the-scenes system health (the data-collection engine). Not about your rankings.

---

## 5. The lifecycle of a keyword (the simple version)

1. **New** — we add the keyword and get its first ranking.
2. **Ongoing** — we optimize; you watch it climb over the bi-weekly checks.
3. **Won (Top 3)** — it reaches the top three and holds. We keep monitoring it.
4. If a won keyword ever slips out of the top 3, it goes back into active optimization.

This keeps effort focused on the keywords that still need to climb, while
protecting the ones you've already won.

---

## 6. What "good" looks like

- More keywords **in the Top 3** over time.
- Keywords moving **up (↑)** across the bi-weekly checks.
- A growing list of **Won** keywords.

That's the whole story: _more of your keywords showing up, higher, in more AI
answers — checked every two weeks so you can see the progress._
